about us

Every day is a chance to become better

Gym and Fitness was founded as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality gym equipment at great prices. Instead of being just another gym equipment retailer, we wanted to be the best in the industry and set their minds to doing so! Over the last two decades we have grown into one of India's largest online fitness equipment retailers, helping thousands of customers live longer, happier and healthier lives.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

To offer the best equipment, range of classes, knowledgeable staff and personal advice in a welcoming environment.
